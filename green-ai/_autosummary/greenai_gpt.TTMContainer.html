greenai_gpt.TTMContainer¶

class greenai_gpt.TTMContainer(dims: List[Tuple[int, int]], rank_or_ranks: Union[int, List[int]])¶

Creates object which stores sequence of TTM cores in a form of PyTorch ParameterList.

Parameters:

  • dims (List[Tuple[int, int]]) – Dimensions of TTM cores.

  • ranks (Union[int, List[int]]) – Ranks of TTM cores.

Variables:

  • ranks (List[Tuple[int, int]]) – Dimensions of TTM cores.

  • cores (nn.ParameterList) – List of torch.nn objects, corresponding to TT cores.
